Feyaad Allie

I'm a Ph.D. Candidate in Political Science at Stanford University. I'm also a 2017 National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellow, a Stanford EDGE Doctoral Fellow, and an APSA Minority Fellow.

My research focuses on the politics of marginalized groups, particularly religious minorities. I also work on projects related to intergroup violence, the intersection of technology and politics and migration. Regionally, most of my work is focused on South Asia.

I am a graduate affiliate with the Immigration Policy Lab and Abbasi Program in Islamic Studies.

Prior to graduate school, I worked on an international development project in Nairobi, Kenya. I received my B.A. summa cum laude in Government from Dartmouth College.
