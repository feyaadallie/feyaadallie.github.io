Feyaad Allie

I'm a Ph.D. Candidate in Political Science at Stanford University focusing on comparative politics, international relations and quantitative methods. I am also a 2017 National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellow and an Stanford Enhancing Diversity in Graduate Education (EDGE) Doctoral Fellow.

My research centers on the politics of marginalized groups including religious minorities and migrants. I also work on projects related to political violence and British colonial rule. Regionally, most of my work is focused on South Asia.

I am a graduate affiliate with the Immigration Policy Lab and Abbasi Program in Islamic Studies.

Prior to graduate school, I worked on an international development project in Nairobi, Kenya. I received my B.A. summa cum laude in Government from Dartmouth College.
