Feyaad Allie

Welcome! I'm a Ph.D. Candidate in Political Science at Stanford University. I am also a 2017 National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellow and Stanford Enhancing Diversity in Graduate Education (EDGE) doctoral fellow.

My research is in comparative politics broadly focusing on representation, political/religious violence, and migration. Regionally, most of my work is focused on South Asia.

I am a graduate affiliate with the Immigration Policy Lab and Abbasi Program in Islamic Studies.

Prior to graduate school, I worked on an international development project in Nairobi, Kenya. I received my B.A. summa cum laude in Government from Dartmouth College.
